## 功能介绍

## 调试

您可以在[API Explorer](https://apiexplorer.developer.huaweicloud.com/apiexplorer/doc?product=APIG&api=CreateGatewayResponseV2)中调试该接口。

## URI

POST /v2/\{project\_id\}/apigw/instances/\{instance\_id\}/api-groups/\{group\_id\}/gateway-responses
